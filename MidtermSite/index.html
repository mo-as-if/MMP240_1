About Pure Leaves

We are a small-business style, Organic Cannabis Dispensary situated in downtown Seattle, WA. We are a family owned establishment, run by two brothers who have an extensive background in botany and sustainable grow operations. With a prime focus in organic farming, we back our products with a guaranteed high quality stamp of approval.
